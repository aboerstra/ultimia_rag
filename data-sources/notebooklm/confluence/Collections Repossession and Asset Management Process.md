<h2><strong>1. Overview</strong></h2><p>The <strong>Collections Process</strong> ensures timely payment tracking, delinquency management, and repossession procedures for Maxim Commercial Capital. It involves:<br />‚úÖ <strong>Tracking overdue accounts</strong> in <strong>LeaseWorks</strong> and <strong>Salesforce (SFDC)</strong>.<br />‚úÖ <strong>Communicating with customers</strong> to resolve delinquencies.<br />‚úÖ <strong>Coordinating repossessions</strong> if necessary.<br />‚úÖ <strong>Managing assets post-repossession</strong> to prepare them for resale.</p><hr /><h2><strong>2. Collections Process</strong></h2><h3><strong>2.1 LeaseWorks Platform &ndash; Tracking Delinquencies</strong></h3><ul><li><p>Once an <strong>account is boarded into LeaseWorks</strong>, the system:</p><ul><li><p><strong>Tracks due dates</strong> for payments.</p></li><li><p>Registers <strong>missed payments</strong>, displaying <strong>days past due</strong> and applicable <strong>late fees</strong>.</p></li><li><p><strong>Creates a queue of delinquent accounts</strong> for collectors to follow up.</p></li></ul></li></ul><hr /><h3><strong>2.2 Collector Actions &ndash; Managing Delinquent Accounts</strong></h3><ul><li><p><strong>Outbound and Inbound Calls:</strong></p><ul><li><p><strong>Collections staff contact customers</strong> about missed payments.</p></li><li><p>They log <strong>all call notes and comments</strong> in LeaseWorks.</p></li></ul></li><li><p><strong>Customer Follow-Up Methods:</strong></p><ul><li><p><strong>Phone calls</strong></p></li><li><p><strong>Text messages</strong> (including customer&rsquo;s name, vehicle, and number of days past due).</p></li><li><p><strong>Emails</strong> requesting payments.</p></li></ul></li><li><p><strong>Promise to Pay Agreements:</strong></p><ul><li><p>If a customer <strong>promises to pay</strong> on a specific date, it is logged in <strong>LeaseWorks</strong>.</p></li><li><p>However, there is <strong>no tracking history of past promises</strong>, making it difficult to monitor recurring issues.</p></li></ul></li><li><p><strong>Tracking Delinquency:</strong></p><ul><li><p><strong>Orion provides reporting</strong> on collection efforts and transactions.</p></li></ul></li></ul><hr /><h3><strong>2.3 GPS Tracking &amp; Communication with Asset Management</strong></h3><ul><li><p><strong>Collectors log into GPS tracking systems</strong> (Passtime, Spireon/GoldStar) to:</p><ul><li><p><strong>Check vehicle location and movement history.</strong></p></li><li><p><strong>Determine asset recovery feasibility.</strong></p></li><li><p><strong>Provide data to the Asset Management team</strong> for repossession decisions.</p></li></ul></li><li><p><strong>Collectors coordinate with Asset Management</strong> to determine:</p><ul><li><p>Whether the customer should get <strong>another chance</strong>.</p></li><li><p>If repossession should proceed.</p></li></ul></li></ul><hr /><h2><strong>3. Repossession Process</strong></h2><h3><strong>3.1 Initiating Repossession</strong></h3><ul><li><p>The <strong>repossession process begins</strong> when a <strong>collector or rep</strong> receives an email notification about a <strong>repo request</strong>.</p></li><li><p><strong>Vehicle Record Updates in SFDC:</strong></p><ul><li><p>The rep <strong>locates the Vehicle record</strong> in Salesforce.</p></li><li><p>The <strong>Disposition field is changed to &quot;PENDING&quot;.</strong></p></li><li><p>The rep <strong>reviews notes in Salesforce</strong> related to the customer&rsquo;s account and payment history.</p></li></ul></li></ul><hr /><h3><strong>3.2 Repossession Authorization &amp; Execution</strong></h3><ul><li><p><strong>Evaluating Repossession Feasibility:</strong></p><ul><li><p>The rep <strong>reviews the vehicle&rsquo;s details, market value, and condition</strong>.</p></li><li><p>If the asset is still <strong>valuable for resale</strong>, the repossession order proceeds.</p></li></ul></li><li><p><strong>Repo Agent Coordination:</strong></p><ul><li><p>The rep <strong>sends an email to the REPO Agent</strong> with order details.</p></li><li><p>Required information includes:</p><ul><li><p><strong>Repo type</strong> (voluntary/involuntary).</p></li><li><p><strong>Order date</strong>.</p></li><li><p><strong>Number of payments made</strong>.</p></li><li><p><strong>GPS tracking status</strong>.</p></li><li><p><strong>Days past due</strong>.</p></li></ul></li></ul></li><li><p><strong>Data Compilation &amp; System Updates:</strong></p><ul><li><p>The rep <strong>compiles repossession data in Excel</strong>.</p></li><li><p><strong>All repossession details are updated in SFDC</strong> for tracking.</p></li></ul></li></ul><hr /><h3><strong>3.3 Post-Repossession Processing</strong></h3><ul><li><p><strong>Vehicle Inspection &amp; Repairs:</strong></p><ul><li><p>The <strong>FLEETROCK system is used</strong> to track <strong>damaged repossessed vehicles</strong> that require repairs.</p></li><li><p>The repair shop <strong>provides an inspection report</strong> detailing necessary fixes.</p></li><li><p>Reps <strong>coordinate repairs and transportation</strong> of the asset to a holding facility.</p></li></ul></li><li><p><strong>Title Verification:</strong></p><ul><li><p><strong>Repo'd trucks must have a title to be resold.</strong></p></li><li><p>Title records are <strong>updated in Salesforce</strong> to reflect ownership status.</p></li></ul></li><li><p><strong>Resale Preparation:</strong></p><ul><li><p>The rep <strong>updates SALE details in SFDC</strong>.</p></li><li><p>An <strong>invoice is generated</strong> using an automated <strong>Salesforce function</strong>.</p></li><li><p>The <strong>REPO TITLE SECTION is updated</strong> to reflect resale readiness.</p></li></ul></li></ul><hr /><h2><strong>4. Asset Management</strong></h2><h3><strong>4.1 Work Order Processing</strong></h3><ul><li><p><strong>Work orders for repairs and repossession handling</strong> are updated in <strong>Salesforce</strong>.</p></li><li><p><strong>Reps track open work orders</strong> using Salesforce-generated reports.</p></li><li><p><strong>Once work orders are approved</strong>, they are automatically <strong>sent to Accounting for payment</strong> via <strong>Salesforce email automation</strong>.</p></li></ul><hr /><h3><strong>4.2 Post-Repair &amp; Inventory Management</strong></h3><ul><li><p>After repairs are complete, the <strong>Asset Manager updates the vehicle's condition to &quot;Inventory&quot;</strong> in SFDC.</p></li><li><p>The vehicle is now <strong>ready for resale</strong>.</p></li></ul><hr /><h3><strong>4.3 Final Review &amp; Sale Completion</strong></h3><ul><li><p><strong>After a truck is sold</strong>, the Asset Manager <strong>conducts a final account review</strong>.</p></li><li><p><strong>Orion provides post-sale reporting</strong> on collections and transactions.</p></li></ul><hr /><h2><strong>5.
