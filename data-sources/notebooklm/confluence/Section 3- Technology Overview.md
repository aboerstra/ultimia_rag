<p /><hr /><h1><strong>Maxim Commercial Capital &ndash; Systems Overview</strong></h1><h2><strong>1. Core Business Systems</strong></h2><h3><strong>1.1 Salesforce (SFDC) &ndash; CRM &amp; Process Management</strong></h3><ul><li><p><strong>Role:</strong> Acts as the <strong>central CRM platform</strong> for managing leads, opportunities, customer information, and document workflows.</p></li><li><p><strong>Key Functions:</strong></p><ul><li><p><strong>Lead and Opportunity Management:</strong> Tracks applications, approvals, and funding stages.</p></li><li><p><strong>Customer Data Storage:</strong> Stores applicant and vehicle information.</p></li><li><p><strong>Approval &amp; Terms Processing:</strong> Terms are generated and approvals are sent via Salesforce.</p></li><li><p><strong>Document Management &amp; Generation:</strong></p><ul><li><p>Uses <strong>Conga Composer</strong> for merging and formatting documents.</p></li><li><p>Funding checklists are generated within Salesforce.</p></li></ul></li><li><p><strong>System Integration Needs:</strong></p><ul><li><p><strong>LeaseWorks Integration</strong> (Manual entry of LeaseWorks Number, updates after booking).</p></li><li><p><strong>GPS Tracking Integration</strong> (Planned connection with Passtime/Spireon for automatic tracking).</p></li><li><p><strong>Bank and Address Verification Automation</strong> (Automate address and account validation within SFDC).</p></li><li><p><strong>Vehicle Valuation Integration</strong> (Integration with JD Power/NADA for vehicle price assessments).</p></li></ul></li></ul></li></ul><hr /><h3><strong>1.2 IFS LeaseWorks &ndash;
