<h2><strong>1. Overview</strong></h2><ul><li><p>The <strong>Booking and Funding process</strong> involves finalizing the financing agreement and <strong>disbursing funds</strong> to the applicant.</p></li></ul><hr /><h2><strong>1. GPS Installation</strong></h2><h3><strong>1.1 GPS Requirement</strong></h3><p>If Dealer did not allow installation of GPS until after Funding</p><ul><li><p><strong>GPS installation is mandatory</strong> for every financed vehicle.</p></li><li><p>The GPS process typically occurs <strong>before funding</strong> unless the <strong>dealer requires installation after funding</strong>.</p></li></ul><h3><strong>1.2 GPS Location Verification</strong></h3><ul><li><p>The vehicle&rsquo;s <strong>GPS location is pinged</strong>, and its <strong>location is attached to the Opportunity</strong> in Salesforce.</p></li></ul><hr /><h2><strong>2. Funding Packet </strong></h2><p>1.1 Funding packet sent from Funding manager to Dealer/Broker/Applicant and Accounting.</p><hr /><h2><strong>3. LeaseWorks Integration and Booking</strong></h2><h3><strong>3.1 Updating Salesforce with LeaseWorks Information</strong></h3><ul><li><p>The <strong>rep updates the Salesforce Opportunity</strong> with the assigned <strong>LeaseWorks number</strong>.</p></li></ul><h3><strong>3.2 Submitting the Funding Packet</strong></h3><ul><li><p>The <strong>Funding Packet is emailed to Accounting</strong>, signaling that it is ready for booking.</p></li><li><p><strong>Accounting retrieves the Funding Package</strong> and proceeds with the next steps.</p></li></ul><h3><strong>3.3 Document Processing and Verification</strong></h3><ul><li><p>The rep:</p><ul><li><p><strong>Performs a document merge</strong> in <strong>Conga Composer</strong>.</p></li><li><p><strong>Opens and verifies an Excel document</strong> generated from the funding data.</p></li><li><p>Clicks <strong>&quot;GET TEXT FILE&quot;</strong> on the Excel document, saves it, renames it, and <strong>uploads it via Serv-U</strong> to input into <strong>LeaseWorks</strong>.</p></li></ul></li></ul><h3><strong>3.4 LeaseWorks Validation</strong></h3><ul><li><p>The <strong>Excel data is cross-checked</strong> against the <strong>Funding Packet</strong>.</p></li><li><p>In case of discrepancies, the <strong>Funding Packet takes precedence</strong> over the Excel data.</p></li></ul><h3><strong>3.5 Booking the Deal in LeaseWorks</strong></h3><ul><li><p>The rep:</p><ul><li><p><strong>Finds the created Application</strong> in LeaseWorks using the <strong>400 number</strong>.</p></li><li><p>Clicks <strong>&quot;Compile&quot;</strong> in LeaseWorks to confirm <strong>D&amp;A (Due &amp; Accrued) certification</strong>.</p></li><li><p>Manually enters the <strong>Contact Role email</strong> into LeaseWorks for <strong>audit tracking</strong>.</p></li><li><p>Clicks <strong>&quot;Book Deal&quot;</strong> to finalize the transaction.</p></li></ul></li></ul><h3><strong>3.6 Final Contract Verification</strong></h3><ul><li><p>Under <strong>Contract &gt; Event</strong>, the <strong>bank account number</strong> is <strong>double-checked against the void check</strong> for accuracy.</p></li><li><p>The <strong>finalized Booking Document is uploaded to Box</strong>.</p></li></ul><hr /><h2><strong>4. Disbursement of Funds</strong></h2><h3><strong>4.1 Preparing for Disbursement</strong></h3><ul><li><p>Once <strong>all stipulations are cleared</strong>, the funding team:</p><ul><li><p><strong>Finalizes the Funding Packet file</strong>.</p></li><li><p><strong>Uploads it to Box</strong> for record-keeping.</p></li></ul></li></ul><h3><strong>4.2 Releasing Funds</strong></h3><ul><li><p><strong>Accounting receives an email</strong> with the <strong>Funding Package in PDF format</strong>, signaling that the deal is ready for payment.</p></li><li><p>The <strong>deal is officially booked into IFS LeaseWorks</strong>, completing the funding process.</p></li></ul><h3><strong>4.3 Manual vs. Automated Data Transfer</strong></h3><ul><li><p>Currently, <strong>Salesforce to LeaseWorks data transfer is manual</strong>, requiring duplicate data entry.</p></li><li><p>Plans are in place to <strong>automate this integration</strong>, reducing manual effort.</p></li><li><p><strong>Nathan</strong> (internal team member) currently replicates Salesforce data manually in LeaseWorks.</p></li></ul><hr /><h2><strong>Systems Used in Booking and Post-Funding Process</strong></h2><h3><strong>8.1 Organizing Funded Opportunities</strong></h3><ul><li><p><strong>Funded Opportunities are moved to the &quot;Funded&quot; folder in Box</strong> for /><h2><strong>10. Conclusion</strong></h2><p>The <strong>Booking and Funding Process</strong> is a <strong>critical stage</strong> that ensures proper documentation, compliance, and secure fund disbursement. However, <strong>manual inefficiencies</strong> in <strong>data entry, document handling, and verification</strong> introduce delays. Automating <strong>Salesforce-to-LeaseWorks integration</strong>, <strong>standardizing document submission</strong>, and <strong>improving checklist management</strong> would significantly <strong>enhance operational efficiency and reduce processing time</strong>.</p><hr /><p />
